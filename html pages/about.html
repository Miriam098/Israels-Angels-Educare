About Us...

Tersia Williams, founder and one of the members of Israel's Angels Educare Center in Hangberg Hout Bay started the centre from a life long dream and inspired by the passing of her 10 month old baby boy on th 10 of July 2017. With the help of her colleagues who she worked with at a former ECD center, they started with the passion to look after children and teach them and the income they made from selling boerewors rolls, hamburgers and Braai platters. With qualification and experience in the ECD field they were confident enough to start their centre and even had a confirmed list of children on their register!

Tersia and her colleagues started with an average of 30 children before opening and on the 1st of June 2018 an estimated of R12 000 with weekly profit of the Mama kind se Braai of roughly R1500 could afford to rent a bigger premises to accomodate more than 60 children, keep the center and the braai stand running. The braai stand enabled them to meet all the needs of the ECD centre.
