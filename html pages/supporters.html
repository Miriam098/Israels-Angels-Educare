Gracie - Love In A Bowl

"A beautiful garden in Hout Bay is feeding child & elderly households"

Gracie Garden - Love in a Bowl grows organic vegetables to donate to the very young & vulnerable & the elderly who are sick. We currently provide approximately 180 000 nutritious vegetarian meals per year to our precious neighbours.

We would just like to thank Gracie Garden - Love in a Bowl for their Generosity! We, at Israel's Angels Educare greatly appreciate your donations & sacrifice. Your continued support helps to further our mission to help & support our parents with veggies and also provide healthy meals to our angles. Together we are making a difference. We can't thank you enough for your valuable support.
Sincerely,
Israel's Angels Educare.
